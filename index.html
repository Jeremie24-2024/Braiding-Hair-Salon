About Us

Nysha Hair Braiding Salon is led by Nysha, a highly skilled braiding specialist with over a decade of professional experience. She is deeply rooted in traditional African braiding techniques and has expertly incorporated modern American styles, including the increasingly popular knotless braids.

Nysha and her team are recognized for their speed, precision, and consistently high‑quality results, no matter the style or hair type. Every client receives personalized care, ensuring each braid is neat, long-lasting, and tailored to your natural beauty and confidence.

Our philosophy is simple and client‑focused: you share your vision, and we bring it to life. With skilled hands and an eye for detail, we ensure every style is crafted to enhance your confidence and celebrate your unique beauty.

Braiding Services

Cornrows▼
Classic, clean & versatile — perfect for any occasion. Intricate patterns that lay flat against the scalp, offering a timeless protective style that lasts 2-4 weeks.
Box Braids▼
Timeless protective style, lightweight and low maintenance. Individual plaits that can be styled in various lengths and thicknesses, perfect for versatility.
Senegalese Twists▼
Elegant, lightweight twists with natural flow. Rope-like twists that offer a sophisticated look with excellent movement and durability.
Knotless Braids▼
Tension‑free, natural-looking — modern classic. Gentle on the scalp with a seamless natural appearance, reducing tension and promoting healthy hair growth.
Faux Locs▼
Bold, stylish, versatile for edgy elegance. Achieve the look of mature locs without the long-term commitment, perfect for a bold style statement.
Crochet Styles▼
Fast, flexible & fully customizable. Quick installation with endless styling options, perfect for those wanting variety and easy maintenance.
